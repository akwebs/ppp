Our Goal is to live in harmony with nature

Dear friends,

We have only one home to live in! We share it with 7.8 billion fellow human beings, nearly 1.2 million cataloged species of plants and animals and estimated 7.5 million eukaryotic species – yet to be discovered.

Beyond doubt, everything and everyone is intricately interconnected that the existence of one depends upon the other and on the fine balance of biotic and abiotic component of the ecosystem. Mother nature has envisaged and ensured it since 4.6 billion years, since our home Planet Earth came into existence.

Unfortunately, the greed of our species has put the mere existence of every other species on the edge, including our own. We human beings are consuming earth resources too fast – faster than they can be replenished. This reckless consumption is depleting the world’s natural capital. This global overshoot is growing and, as a consequence, ecosystems are being run down and waste is accumulating in the air, land, and water. The resulting deforestation, global warming, water shortages, declining biodiversity, habitat loss and climate change is endangering our future.

We human beings have a remarkable track record of ingenuity and problem solving. The same spirit that took us to the moon must now be harnessed to free future generations from ecological crises. We need to pledge to stop the degradation of the planet’s natural environment and to build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature.

Let us begin to live within the means of the planet, while securing our own well-being and the ecosystems on which we depend. Let us individual decide and work to reduce his/her own ecological footprint, so that, this micro eco-management add up and the entire human economy operates within the Earth’s ecological limits. If we have the will, we have the way.

Kapil Gupta
